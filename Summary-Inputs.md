Summary Inputs.

Throughout the production behind this program, the health risk calculator has provided inputs for the user. Age simply allows the user to type in their age number. Also a text box to type the numbers of both feet and inches of height as well as weight(lbs). Drop downs are added for both the blood pressure and family history. This allows the user to choose (depending on the option) where their blood pressure is at and the family history. Finally, the calculate risk button. Once the user goes through all the inputs, the last input is simply calculating the risk of their blood pressure. Depending on their input the results are low risk, moderate risk, high risk or uninsurable. To conclude, these are the inputs within the health risk calculator, for the user(s). - Sadri
